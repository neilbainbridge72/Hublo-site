How it works

From first contact to
network partner.

01
First contact
We find you
We may reach out when one of our guests needs accommodation in your area. We'll check your property is a good fit — location, spec, availability — and have a friendly conversation about pricing for the stay.
02
First booking
We place a guest with you
If it's a good match, we'll make the booking. We handle all the communication and logistics. Your job is simply to provide a great stay for the guest we send.

After a successful stay, we'll invite you to join our network — completely free. As a network partner, you'll be our first call whenever a guest needs accommodation in your area. Consistent, direct, commission-free bookings.
