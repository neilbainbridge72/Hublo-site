1. About Hublo

Hublo Ltd is a company registered in England and Wales under company registration number 17098088. Our registered office is at 7 Westmead Trading Estate, Westmead, Swindon, SN5 7YS.

Hublo Ltd operates as an accommodation booking agency, acting as an intermediary between guests seeking accommodation and accommodation providers. We do not own the properties we book and are not a party to any accommodation agreement between a guest and a provider.

  • "Provider" refers to any individual or organisation that offers accommodation that is booked through Hublo.

  • "Network" refers to the group of accommodation providers registered directly with Hublo.

3. Our Services

Hublo provides an accommodation booking and concierge service operating nationally across the United Kingdom. Our services include:

  • Searching for suitable accommodation based on the client's requirements
  • Vetting properties for suitability, quality and value
  • Negotiating rates with accommodation providers on the client's behalf
  • Making reservations and managing booking confirmations
  • Communicating relevant booking information to the client
  • Acting as the primary point of contact throughout the booking process

Hublo acts as an agent on behalf of the client. The accommodation contract is between the client and the provider. Hublo is not responsible for the provision of the accommodation itself.

4. Booking Process

All bookings are subject to availability and confirmation by the accommodation provider. A booking is not confirmed until Hublo has provided the client with written confirmation of the reservation.

The client is responsible for ensuring that the information provided to Hublo (including dates, number of guests, location, and any specific requirements) is accurate at the time of enquiry. Hublo accepts no liability for incorrect bookings arising from inaccurate information provided by the client.

Hublo reserves the right to decline an enquiry or cancel a booking at its discretion, including where a suitable property cannot be sourced within the client's requirements.

5. Fees and Payment

Hublo's fees are incorporated into the accommodation rate presented to the client. There are no additional booking fees charged to the client unless otherwise agreed in writing prior to a booking being made.

Payment terms will be agreed at the time of booking confirmation. Hublo reserves the right to require a deposit or full payment in advance for certain bookings.

All prices quoted are inclusive of VAT where applicable.

Hublo reserves the right to amend its pricing at any time. Any changes to agreed pricing will be communicated to the client prior to any booking being confirmed.

6. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations by the Client

If a client wishes to cancel a confirmed booking, they must notify Hublo in writing as soon as possible. Cancellation charges may apply depending on the notice period given and the terms agreed with the accommodation provider at the time of booking. These will be communicated to the client at the time of confirmation.

As a general guide, the following cancellation terms apply unless otherwise stated at the time of booking:

  • More than 14 days' notice — no cancellation charge
  • 7 to 14 days' notice — 50% of the total booking cost
  • Less than 7 days' notice — 100% of the total booking cost

Hublo will always endeavour to negotiate the most favourable cancellation terms on the client's behalf.

Cancellations by the Provider

In the event that an accommodation provider cancels a confirmed booking, Hublo will endeavour to source suitable alternative accommodation as quickly as possible. Where alternative accommodation cannot be sourced, a full refund will be issued to the client.

Cancellations by Hublo

Hublo reserves the right to cancel a booking in exceptional circumstances, including where there is evidence of fraudulent activity or misrepresentation by the client. In such cases, a full refund minus any non-recoverable costs will be issued.

7. Refund Policy

Refunds will be processed within 14 working days of a cancellation being confirmed. Refunds will be made via the original payment method wherever possible.

Where a booking is cancelled by the provider, Hublo will issue a full refund of any amounts paid by the client.

Where a client cancels a booking, refunds will be issued in accordance with the cancellation policy set out in Section 6.

Hublo will not be liable for any indirect losses arising from a cancellation, including but not limited to travel costs, loss of earnings, or any other consequential losses.
